Common questions
What exactly does the Document Checker do? ↓
You paste the text of any contract, terms and conditions, tenancy agreement, employment clause, or legal letter. The Document Checker gives you: a plain-English summary of what it actually says, red/amber/green flags highlighting unfair or unusual clauses, your specific rights under UK law, an overall fairness rating, and clear steps on what to do.
Why is the Document Checker not free? ↓
Detailed document analysis uses significantly more AI processing than a simple question and answer. Keeping it as a paid feature means we can keep everything else — guides, AI assistant, tools — completely free with no ads.
What types of documents can I analyse? ↓
Employment contracts, tenancy agreements, service contracts, freelance agreements, app terms and conditions, privacy policies, refund policies, supplier contracts, NDAs, debt collection letters, or any specific clause you want explained in plain English.
Is it real legal advice? ↓
No. The Document Checker provides general legal information based on UK law — not advice from a qualified solicitor. For high-value or complex matters, always consult a solicitor via solicitors.lawsociety.org.uk.
Can I cancel anytime? ↓
Yes — cancel through your PayPal account at any time, no fees, no questions asked. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.
